Read Before Booking

Studio Policies

Please read fully before requesting an appointment — this keeps things fair and running smoothly for every girl on the books.

💗 Late Fee Policy

+
  • Running 10 minutes late — a $10 late fee applies.
  • More than 15 minutes late — your appointment will be canceled and the deposit is forfeited.
  • Once you're on your way, please keep me updated 🚗 — text when you're about 5 minutes out so I can meet you at your vehicle.

💗 Deposits & Booking

+
  • A $15 non-refundable deposit is required to secure every appointment.
  • House call appointments carry an additional $30 travel fee.
  • No-call no-shows forfeit the deposit and may be asked to prepay in full for future bookings.

💗 Foreign Fills

+
  • Foreign fills (sets started by another lash tech) are accepted.
  • When booking, please provide: your lash sizes/mm, the style you'd like, and clear photos or video of your current set.
  • Let me know the diameter your previous lash tech used, and whether it's hybrid or classic.
  • Foreign fills are priced higher than a regular fill since it isn't my original work — final quote is confirmed after photos are reviewed.

Care Guide

Before & After Your Appointment

Follow this and your set will thank you — longer retention, healthier natural lashes, happier RiRi.

💗 Routine Care

Come in with:

  • Clean lashes — no mascara, oil, or makeup residue.
  • Freshly showered if possible — oily lids affect adhesive bond.
  • No caffeine right before your appointment (it makes your eyes flutter more!).
  • Contacts out or in a case handy if you wear them.

For the first 24–48 hours:

  • Avoid water, steam, and sweat on the lash line.
  • No mascara, oil-based products, or makeup remover near the eyes.
  • Sleep on your back or use a lash pillow to protect the set.

Ongoing:

  • Cleanse lashes daily with a lash-safe cleanser to extend retention.
  • Brush through gently every morning.
  • Book your fill at 2–3 weeks max for the healthiest natural lash underneath.
